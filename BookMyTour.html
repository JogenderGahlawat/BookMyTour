To make the most of online travel booking, consider being flexible with travel dates as this can significantly reduce costs. Using multiple flight and hotel search engines to compare prices is recommended. Booking in advance often leads to lower fares, especially during peak seasons, although prices can fluctuate. It's worth considering package deals that bundle flights and hotels for potential savings. Additionally, checking reviews and ratings from other travelers provides valuable insights before making a booking decision. Remember to browse in incognito mode on your web browser to prevent websites from tracking your searches and potentially inflating prices based on your Browse history.
